Translation Tool

I used scans of Anne Lister's diary from here to create a font which resembles her handwriting. The key for her crypthand can be seen here and here. The text entered is translated appropriately to Anne Lister's crypthand, but note that special characters, numbers, and punctuation will not be included in the translation, as they are not included in her code. I do show spaces between the words for ease of understanding, but Anne did not include and spaces in her crypthand.
